> _This program can perform the following conversion:_

- Binary to Decimal
- Binary to Octal
- Binary to Hexa-Decimal
- Decimal to Binary
- Decimal to Octal
- Decimal to Hexa-Decimal
- Octal to Binary
- Octal to Decimal
- Octal to Hexa-Decimal
- Hexa-Decimal to Binary
- Hexa-Decimal to Decimal
- Hexa-Decimal to Octal
